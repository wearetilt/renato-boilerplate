Allyship like art is not linear and not monochromatic.

Allyship is fluid, with ebbs and flows, and looks different for everyone and every situation-

making each person's journey as an ally a beautiful work of art.

While there is no one way to put allyship into action, it is everyone's responsibility. The year of allyship is dedicated to this discovery.

Allyship is a personal responsibility and life-long journey.

It is the practice of empathizing with the experiences of systemically-disadvantaged groups, looking out for biases, and actively using a voice and power to advance equity.

Allyship is not a destination or a self-assessed title; others must see an individual as an ally.

Discover what it means to practice allyship, and consider how comfortable you are with these allyship behaviors.
